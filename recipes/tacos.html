Tacos

Description:

This recipe is especially tailored to those with Celiac disease as it is gluten-free. This is a delicious recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-Free Corn Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Chili Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Powder
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Toppings

Steps:

  1. Cook ground beef, break into small pieces.
  2. Drain fat from frying pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and spices, mix well.
  4. Remove heat mixture and assemble tacos.
  5. Slightly heat the tortillas.
  6. Fill with ground beed and toppings.
  7. ENJOY THE TACOS!
