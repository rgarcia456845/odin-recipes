Easy Gluten Free Lasagna

Description:

This is a special dish that is prepared for those with a form of gluten intolerance such as those with a wheat allergy or with Celiac Disease.

Ingredients:

  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Dried Parsley
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricotta/Cottage Cheese
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauces
  • Gluten-Free Lasagna Noodles

Steps:

  1. Mix Cheese Micture
  2. Brown Onions/Meat
  3. Add Sauce
  4. Spread Sauce on Bottom of Pan
  5. Coat Noodles in Sauce
  6. Layer Noodles
  7. Add Dollops of Cheese
  8. Spread Cheese into Even Layer-Repeat
