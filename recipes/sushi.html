Gluten-Free Sushi Recipe

Description:

This is a recipe for a delicious batch of gluten free sushi rolls.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Sugar
  • Seaweed
  • Carrots
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Toasted Tofu
  • Sesame Oil
  • Toasted Sesame
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Make the Rice
  2. Assemble the sushi
  3. Roll the nori rolls (Seaweed)
  4. Slice the Sushi
  5. Serve the Sushi
  6. (Optional)Dip in Soy Sauce
